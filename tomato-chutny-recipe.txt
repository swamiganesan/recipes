Ingredients:
 - 2 cups of sweet baby tomatoes
 - 1 tea spoon of sambar powder
 - 3/4 tea spoon of masala
 - salt to taste
 - 1 white onion
 - 1 dry red chili


Directions:
 1. sautee the onion with the dry red chili
 
 2. add whole cheeery tomatoes and half a cup of water and let it cook. 

 3. Add the sambar powder and masala powder after a few mins

 4. Let it cook down to a chutney /thick consistency 

 3. Add salt to taste
